Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a book?

Visit the circulation desk with your student ID. Our librarians will help you locate and check out materials.

What is the lending period?

Standard borrowing period is 14 days for most materials. Extensions are available upon request.

How do I access e-resources?

Use your student credentials to log in through the library portal for remote access to digital resources.

Is there a late fee?

Yes, late fees are Ksh 10 per day per item. Items become overdue after the lending period expires.

What are the library's operating hours during exam period?

During examination periods, the library extends its hours to 24/7 access to accommodate student needs.

How do I reserve a study room?

Study rooms can be booked online through the library portal or at the information desk for up to 2 hours per session.

Does the library offer printing services?

Yes, printing services are available at Ksh 5 per page for black and white, and Ksh 15 for color prints.

How can I access past examination papers?

Past papers are available in digital format through the library's institutional repository and in print at the reference section.

Is Wi-Fi available in the library?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the library. Connect using your student credentials.

How do I get research assistance?

Librarians are available for one-on-one research consultations. You can book an appointment through the library website or visit the reference desk.
